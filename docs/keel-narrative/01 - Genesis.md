## What We Know

- Originally called **mitho-dash**, later renamed to **Keel**
- Built by **Framestudio** as a business management platform for Kenyan small shops
- Two-app architecture: admin dashboard + public mini-catalogue
- Started as a single-app project, then split into separate deployments
- The mini-catalogue template was created to give shop owners a professional public-facing storefront without monthly fees
