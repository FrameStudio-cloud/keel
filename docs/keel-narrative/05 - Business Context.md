## Target Audience

Small retail shops in Kenya, specifically:
- Clothing boutiques
- Electronics/electricals stores
- General shops
- Hair/wig vendors
- Any small business that needs inventory + sales tracking + a public storefront

## Market Context

- Many small shops in Kenya use paper records or simple spreadsheets
- Existing POS systems are expensive (monthly subscriptions, hardware costs)
- WhatsApp is the de facto sales channel — shops post products in groups and take orders via DM
- Most shops don't have a website due to cost and complexity

## Keel's Value Proposition

- **Free** (self-hosted or low-cost Vercel deployment)
- **No monthly fees** — only Supabase and Vercel costs
- **WhatsApp-native** — checkout flow sends order via WhatsApp
- **Multi-tenant** — one installation serves multiple shops
- **Professional storefront** — mini-catalogue looks custom-built

## Development Philosophy

- **Mobile-first** — Most shop owners use phones, not desktops
- **Offline-resilient** — Kenyan internet can be unreliable
- **Simple > feature-rich** — One good workflow per feature, not 10 configurable options
- **No vendor lock-in** — Supabase is open-source, deploy anywhere
